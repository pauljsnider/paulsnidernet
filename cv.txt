Summary
Paul is a results-driven engineering executive and innovation leader, expert at transforming healthcare by shaping and executing strategic technology roadmaps that deliver scalable, secure, and data-driven solutions. With over 20 years in healthcare IT, he has a proven track record of leading large, globally distributed teams (300+ engineers) in delivering mission-critical SaaS platforms for EHR, interoperability, and advanced analytics. As Vice President of Engineering at Netsmart, Paul spearheads the technology vision for cloud-native architectures and AI-driven data platforms, directly impacting business capabilities, market strategy, and achieving key business outcomes for tens of thousands of healthcare organizations.
His career began in pharmacy product development, progressing to senior leadership roles across diverse healthcare domains. Paul excels at integrating complex healthcare ecosystems, strategically leveraging AI/ML for actionable insights, and championing agile methodologies to accelerate innovation and achieve significant business transformation. Prior to Netsmart, he led global platform engineering teams at one of the world's largest EHR providers, spearheading key strategies for interoperability, large-scale cloud deployments, and data-centric solutions that shaped the company's technological direction.
Paul holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems from Missouri State University and a Master of Business Administration from Webster University. His leadership philosophy is rooted in continuous learning, coaching, and a user-focused approach, driving his commitment to creating technology that enhances healthcare accessibility, effectiveness, and efficiency.
