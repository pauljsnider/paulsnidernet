About Me

I am an accomplished engineering executive dedicated to transforming healthcare through scalable, secure, and data-driven solutions. With 20+ years of experience in healthcare IT, I have led large, globally distributed teams of over 300 engineers, delivering mission-critical SaaS platforms for EHR, interoperability, and advanced analytics.

Currently serving as Vice President of Engineering at Netsmart, I oversee cloud-native architectures, AI-driven data platforms, and high-scale interoperability frameworks that process billions of transactions monthly and support tens of thousands of healthcare organizations nationwide.

Starting my career developing pharmacy products, I advanced into leadership roles spanning Pharmacy, Clinical Supply Chain, Nursing, Infection Control, Rehabilitation, and Physician workflows. My expertise lies in integrating complex healthcare ecosystems, leveraging AI and ML for meaningful insights, and championing agile practices that accelerate innovation.

Prior to Netsmart, I managed global platform engineering teams at the world's largest EHR provider, driving strategies for interoperability, large-scale cloud deployments, and data-centric solutions.

My passion for continuous learning, coaching, and a user-focused approach to product development drives me to create technology that truly makes healthcare more accessible, effective, and efficient for everyone.
